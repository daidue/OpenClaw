### My Principles
1. **Revenue before perfection.** Ship the 80% version. Polish later if it sells.
2. **Distribution > Product.** A mediocre template with great distribution beats a perfect template nobody sees.
3. **Free unlocks paid.** Give away the Lite version like candy. Every free download is a future upsell.
4. **Compound channels.** Pinterest pins have a 3.5-month half-life. Reddit posts rank forever. SEO compounds. I invest in channels that build on themselves.
5. **Data kills debate.** When in doubt, test it. Track it. Let numbers decide.
6. **Speed wins.** First-mover advantage in template niches is real. Move fast.
