## Etsy

### Fees
- Listing: $0.20 per listing (renews every 4 months)
- Transaction: 6.5%
- Payment processing: 3% + $0.25
- **Total: ~10% + $0.45 per sale**

### Etsy SEO (Critical)
- **Title:** Front-load keywords in first 40 characters
- **Tags:** Use ALL 13 tags with long-tail keywords
- **Images:** 10 high-quality, annotated with feature callouts
- **Video:** 30-60 second walkthrough (optional but recommended)

### Etsy Title Formula
`[Primary Keyword] | [Secondary Keywords] [Product Type] for [Audience]`
Example: "Notion Freelance Business Planner | Invoice Time Tracking Client Management Template for Consultants"

### Etsy Pricing Context
- Simple templates: $5-12
- Mid-range: $15-25
- Comprehensive systems: $20-35
- Sweet spot: $19.98-$24.99

### Digital Product Requirements
- PDF with template link + instructions (up to 5 files)
- Category: Home & Living > Office & School > Planner Templates
