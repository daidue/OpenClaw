# X Reply Strategy Playbook

Build authentic authority through strategic, value-adding replies.

## Core Strategy

- **Volume:** 40-50 replies/day across tiered target accounts
- **Timing:** 2-3 min delay post-publication (human-like, not bot-like)
- **Value-first:** Add insights, share experiences, ask smart questions
- **Peak windows:** 9-11 AM ET, 1-2 PM ET, 8-10 PM ET

## Daily Reply Allocation

| Tier | Focus | Replies/Day |
|------|-------|-------------|
| 1 | AI Agents & ecosystem | 15 (30%) |
| 2 | Automation & no-code | 10 (20%) |
| 3 | Indie hackers & solopreneurs | 15 (30%) |
| 4 | Freelancing & digital nomads | 5 (10%) |
| 5 | Growth marketing | 5 (10%) |
