- **Send to:** 10-15 freelancer contacts (people you've interacted with, mutuals, or friendly acquaintances)
- **Platforms:** X/Twitter DMs, LinkedIn messages, Discord DMs (where you have existing rapport)
- **When to send:** Stagger over 2-3 days (don't blast all at once)
- **Don't send to:** Cold contacts, people you haven't talked to in 6+ months, or anyone who might find it spammy
- **Goal:** 3-5 downloads + genuine feedback + relationship-building

## Personalization Checklist
Before sending, customize:
- [ ] Use their actual name (not "Name")
- [ ] Reference something recent (their project, a conversation, shared interest)
- [ ] Match their communication style (formal vs casual)
- [ ] If you haven't talked in a while, acknowledge it: "Long time! How've you been?"
- [ ] Make it genuinely easy to say no
- [ ] Give them a SPECIFIC feedback prompt (not just "what do you think?")

---

## Response Handling

**If they try it and give feedback:**
"This is super helpful, thank you! [Respond specifically to their feedback]. Really appreciate you taking the time â€” want me to send you the updated version once I tweak [thing they mentioned]?"

**If they say "I don't use Notion":**
"No worries at all! What do you use for invoice tracking? I'm always curious how other freelancers handle this stuff."

**If they don't respond after 48 hours:**
**Let it go.** Do NOT follow up. They're busy or not interested. Both are fine.

**If they download and love it:**
"Awesome! Mind if I circle back in a couple weeks with the results from this launch? Building more freelance tools and your input helps."

**If they ask about the paid version:**
"Yeah, I have a $27 Professional Toolkit with 6 databases and more email scripts, but honestly this free tracker is solid on its own. Only upgrade if you actually need the extra features."

**If they share it with someone:**
"Oh wow, thank you! That's really kind. Let me know if [person they shared with] has feedback too."

## Success Metrics
- **10-15 messages sent** (don't go over 15)
- **3-5 downloads** (30-50% conversion is realistic among friends)
- **1-2 pieces of actionable feedback** (specific improvements to make)
- **0 people annoyed** (if anyone seems put off, stop immediately and reassess your approach)
- **2-3 relationships strengthened** (this should deepen connections, not extract value)
