## Important Guidelines

**Before Posting:**
1. **Be a member first** ‚Äî Don't join just to drop a link. Engage authentically for 3-5 days first.
2. **Read the rules** ‚Äî Some servers require mod approval for self-promo. Ask first.
3. **Search the server** ‚Äî Has someone else recently shared an invoice tracker? Don't duplicate.
4. **Check channel descriptions** ‚Äî Is this the right channel for templates/resources?
5. **Respect the culture** ‚Äî Some communities are strict about self-promotion, others are open. Follow the vibe.

**After Posting:**
1. **Engage beyond your post** ‚Äî React to others' shares, answer questions, be a real member.
2. **Respond to all comments** within 24 hours (even just "thanks!" for positive reactions).
3. **Don't cross-post immediately** ‚Äî Space out posts across different communities by 24-48 hours.
4. **Be helpful, not defensive** ‚Äî If someone criticizes, thank them and engage genuinely.
5. **If it feels spammy, don't post** ‚Äî When in doubt, ask a mod first.

## Red Flags (When NOT to Post)

- ‚ùå You joined the server within the last week
- ‚ùå The community has had multiple recent template shares (you'll look spammy)
- ‚ùå Server rules explicitly say "no self-promotion" (ask a mod first)
- ‚ùå You haven't contributed anything to the community yet
- ‚ùå Another invoice tracker was shared in the last 2 weeks
- ‚ùå The channel culture is strictly anti-commercial (even for free products)
