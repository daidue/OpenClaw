## üçã LEMON SQUEEZY

### 1. Signup URL and Process

**URL:** https://app.lemonsqueezy.com/register

**Process:**
1. Visit signup page and create account with:
   - Email/password, OR
   - Sign up with Google or Twitter
2. Receive confirmation email and click verification link
3. Create your first store:
   - Choose store name
   - Choose subdomain (e.g., `your-store.lemonsqueezy.com`)
4. Account starts in **Test Mode** - you can test everything before going live
5. When ready to sell:
   - Add payout method (PayPal or bank account)
   - Activate store (requires 1-2 business day review/approval)
   - Copy test products to live mode using "Copy to Live Mode" feature

**Store Activation:** Lemon Squeezy acts as Merchant of Record and reviews each store to ensure compliance with terms and verify products aren't on prohibited list.

### 2. How to Create a Digital Product Listing (Notion Template)

**Navigation:** Store ‚Üí Products ‚Üí Add Product

**Product Types Available:**
- One-off products

- Pay-what-you-want products
- Product variants (different versions/tiers)

**For Notion Templates:**
1. Create new product

**Recommended:** Upload a PDF with:

### 5. Built-in Affiliate Program Setup

**URL:** https://app.lemonsqueezy.com/settings/affiliates

**Setup Process:**
1. Navigate to Settings ‚Üí Affiliates
2. Affiliate program is **already active** by default (optional to use)
3. Configure settings:
   - **Affiliate referral URL:** Default links to your storefront, can change to any domain
   - **Affiliate signup URL:** Share this for affiliates to apply to join
   - **Default commission:** Set percentage or flat rate
   - **Tracking length:** Cookie duration for attribution
   - **Affiliate products:** Select specific products or custom rates per product

**Management:**
- Affiliates must apply to join
- Can set auto-approval or manual review
- Individual commission rates per affiliate
- Built-in tracking (install script if selling outside storefront)
- No tracking script needed if selling only via Lemon Squeezy storefront

**Application Process:**
- Share affiliate signup URL
- Affiliates apply
- You approve/reject applications
- They get unique referral links

### 6. How to Create PWYW/Free Product (Lite Version)

**Product Type:** Pay What You Want

**Setup:**
1. Create new product
2. Select pricing type: "Pay what you want"
3. Configure:
   - **Minimum price:** $0 (for truly free option)
   - **Suggested price:** Set a recommended amount (e.g., $5)
4. At checkout:
   - Suggested price appears in editable field
   - Customers can change amount
   - Cannot pay less than minimum ($0 in our case)
5. Upload Notion template files or redirect URL
6. Great for collecting email addresses

**Strategy for Lite Version:**
- Set minimum to $0
- Set suggested price to $3-5
- Captures email subscribers
- Low barrier to entry
- Upsell opportunity to full $27 toolkit
