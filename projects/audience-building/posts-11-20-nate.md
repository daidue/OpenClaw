## Post 14 — Behind-the-Scenes (Day 25)

People ask: "How do you coordinate 4 agents?"

Answer: I don't. They coordinate themselves.

Each agent has a specialty (research, content, dev, coordination). The lead agent decides who does what based on the task.

My job: Set the goal. Approve the plan. Review the output.

Their job: Everything else.

This week I "worked" 6 hours and shipped a $27 product that took 40+ hours of actual labor. That's the leverage.

## Post 16 — Practical Tip (Day 29)

Want to stress-test an AI agent's value before committing?

Give it this prompt:

*"Find the top 5 competitors in [your niche]. For each one, identify: their pricing strategy, their positioning angle, and one thing they're doing that I should copy or avoid. Deliver in a table."*

Good agent → insight-dense table in 30 minutes.

Bad agent → generic summaries you could've Googled faster.

This single test has saved me $2,000+ in wasted subscriptions.

## Post 18 — Insight (Day 33)

Cost: $0.40 in API calls. Value: priceless.
