# Nate Calloway Newsletter — "The Operator's Log"

## Concept
Weekly newsletter documenting the journey of a customer acquisition director building businesses with AI agents. Real experiments, real numbers, no hype.

## Platform
**Substack** (free to start, built-in discovery, paid tier later)
- URL: natecalloway.substack.com
- Or custom: theoperatorslog.substack.com

## Cadence
Weekly (Sunday evening) — prep for the week ahead

## Issue Structure (~1,500 words)
1. **This Week's Experiment** (500 words) — What we tried, what happened
2. **The Numbers** (200 words) — Revenue, followers, metrics (radical transparency)
3. **One Insight** (300 words) — Deepest lesson from the week
4. **Tool/Skill of the Week** (200 words) — Something useful readers can use immediately
5. **What's Next** (100 words) — Preview of next week's experiment
6. **Links & Resources** (5-7 links) — Best things we found this week
