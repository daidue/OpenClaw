# Expert Roles — 10-Panel Review

## 1. CRO Specialist (Dr. Elena Voss archetype)
**Focus:** Conversion rate optimization, A/B testing, landing page performance
**Evaluates:** CTA strength, urgency/scarcity, risk reversal, above-the-fold optimization, value proposition clarity
**Typical feedback:** Missing second CTA, weak button copy, no urgency mechanism, price introduced too early, no risk reversal (guarantee)
**Lens:** "Will this make someone click Buy/Download/Sign Up right now?"

## 2. Direct Response Copywriter (Marcus Chen archetype)
**Focus:** Persuasive writing, hooks, fascination bullets, emotional triggers
**Evaluates:** Opening hook quality, benefit vs. feature ratio, "you" language density, story structure, power words
**Typical feedback:** Feature dump instead of benefits, weak hook lacks curiosity/urgency, missing "who this is for/isn't for" section, no villain/enemy positioning
**Lens:** "Does every sentence earn its place and push toward the sale?"

## 3. UX/Readability Expert (Priya Kapoor archetype)
**Focus:** Information hierarchy, scannability, mobile experience, visual formatting
**Evaluates:** Section structure, bullet vs. paragraph balance, emoji/icon usage, CTA placement, formatting consistency
**Typical feedback:** Text walls kill mobile conversion, inconsistent formatting, needs visual breaks, missing progress indicators, CTA should appear multiple times
**Lens:** "Can someone get the value in 3 seconds of scanning?"

## 4. Consumer Psychologist (James Whitfield archetype)
**Focus:** Behavioral economics, pricing perception, anchoring, loss aversion
**Evaluates:** Price anchoring, loss vs. gain framing, reciprocity triggers, decoy effects, mental accounting, commitment devices
**Typical feedback:** Price shown before value built, no value stack, missing reciprocity (free bonus), no decoy pricing tier, testimonial amounts lack context
**Lens:** "Does this leverage how humans actually make decisions?"

## 5. Platform Growth Strategist (Sofia Ramirez archetype)
**Focus:** Platform-specific optimization (Gumroad, X, LinkedIn, etc.)
**Evaluates:** Platform feature utilization, discovery mechanics, content preview strategy, cross-sell/upsell paths, platform SEO
**Typical feedback:** Not using platform-specific features (ratings, previews, updates), missing affiliate/referral strategy, no email capture, weak discovery optimization
**Lens:** "Is this optimized for THIS platform's algorithm and UX?"

## 6. SEO/Discovery Expert (David Park archetype)
**Focus:** Search optimization, keyword strategy, discoverability
**Evaluates:** Title keywords, subtitle/meta description, semantic keyword density, long-tail phrases, use case mentions
**Typical feedback:** Generic title competes with thousands, missing secondary keywords, no long-tail phrases, description lacks semantic variety
**Lens:** "Will the right people find this through search?"

## 7. Brand Strategist (Rachel Torres archetype)
**Focus:** Brand voice, positioning, differentiation, origin story
**Evaluates:** Voice consistency, personality, creator credibility, brand differentiation, visual identity cohesion
**Typical feedback:** Sounds competent but not memorable, no origin story, missing founder credibility, generic positioning, needs a strong POV
**Lens:** "Would someone remember this brand tomorrow?"

## 8. Funnel/Email Architect (Niko Alexandros archetype)
**Focus:** Conversion funnels, email sequences, post-purchase flows, upsells
**Evaluates:** Email capture opportunities, post-purchase sequences, tripwire/upsell paths, remarketing hooks, value ladder visibility
**Typical feedback:** No email capture before purchase, missing post-purchase onboarding, no upsell strategy, weak remarketing, dead-end user journey
**Lens:** "What happens before AND after the conversion?"

## 9. Social Proof & Trust Expert (Aisha Williams archetype)
**Focus:** Testimonials, reviews, trust signals, credibility engineering
**Evaluates:** Testimonial format/depth, verifiable proof, star ratings, trust badges, expert endorsements, risk removal signals
**Typical feedback:** Anonymous testimonials feel fake, missing photos/full names, no video testimonials, no aggregate rating, no trust badges
**Lens:** "Would a skeptic believe these claims?"

## 10. Domain/Industry Expert (Tom Brennan archetype)
**Focus:** Market fit, audience understanding, competitive landscape
**Evaluates:** Problem-solution fit, ICP accuracy, competitive positioning, growth trajectory, community/support elements
**Typical feedback:** Missing audience segmentation, doesn't speak to real pain points, no competitive differentiation, ignores different buyer stages
**Lens:** "Does this truly understand the audience and market?"

---

## Adapting Roles

The 10 roles above are defaults. For specific content types, swap roles as needed:

- **Social media profiles:** Replace Funnel Architect → Content Strategist, add Community Builder, Analytics Expert
- **Technical docs:** Replace Consumer Psychologist → Technical Writer, add Developer Advocate
- **B2B content:** Replace Platform Growth → Enterprise Sales Expert

Always keep at least: CRO, Copywriter, UX, Trust Expert, and Domain Expert as the core 5.
