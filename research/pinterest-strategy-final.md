## 3. Pin Design Best Practices

### Committed Brand System *(Tightened for consistency)*

**Every pin MUST include:**
- Brand color block (navy or coral) â€” at least 20% of pin area
- "Jeff the Notion Guy" wordmark â€” bottom left or right, 18-22pt
- Montserrat Bold for headlines, Open Sans for body
- URL display: "jeffthenotionguy.gumroad.com" at bottom

### Optimal Dimensions
- **Standard (80% of pins):** 1000 x 1500 px (2:3)
- **Video:** 1000 x 1500 px, 6-15 seconds, MP4, captions always
- **Carousel:** Up to 5 images, same dimensions

### Text Overlay Rules
- 30-40% text, 60-70% imagery
- Headline: 5-8 words, 60-80pt Montserrat Bold
- CTA: Bottom 1/4 of pin, contrasting color
- Mobile-first: Preview at phone size before publishing

### Accessibility Standards *(NEW)*
- Text contrast: minimum 4.5:1 ratio (WCAG 2.1 AA)
- No critical info in text smaller than 24pt
- Alt text on every pin (keyword-rich description)

### High-Converting Pin Formats

1. **Product Mockup** â€” Template on laptop, clean background, navy/coral palette
2. **Before/After** â€” Chaos (red/gray) â†’ Organized (green/blue) split screen
