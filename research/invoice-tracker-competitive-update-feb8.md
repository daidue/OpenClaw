## Market Insights & Trends (2026)

### 1. Subscription Fatigue is Real
- Freelancers juggling 5-10+ tools at $10-50/month each
- Reddit/forum complaints about costs adding up
- HoneyBook's recent price increase sparked backlash: "too expensive for what I need"

### 2. The "Free vs. Paid" Divide
- Wave's free tier sets a high bar for basic invoicing
- Users only pay for advanced features (automation, integrations, branding)
- **Implication:** We need to differentiate on *workflow integration* (Notion) and *ownership* (no account, no fees)

### 3. All-in-One Fatigue
- Many users report "feature bloat" in HoneyBook, Bonsai
- "I only need invoicing, not a full CRM"
- **Opportunity:** Market as "simple, focused invoice tracking for Notion users"

### 4. Growing Freelance Market
- Source: Technavio report - "significant growth" in freelance platforms, driven by cost-effectiveness
- More freelancers = more potential customers for lightweight, affordable tools
